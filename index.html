Tomatoes, scientifically classified as Solanum lycopersicum, are a captivating and multifaceted member of the nightshade family. Originating in the western regions of South America, tomatoes have evolved from wild, small berry-sized fruits to the diverse array of cultivars we recognize today. These fruits showcase an impressive spectrum of shapes, sizes, and colors, from the tiny and sweet cherry tomatoes to the robust and meaty beefsteak varieties.
